ABOUT VENUE





Live the Lit Life

VENUE. The lit life is all about setting the trends, being connected to everything, and stealing the show wherever you go. The new Hyundai VENUE is
the perfect addition to do all that and more.








Home to car(H2C) with Alexa and Google Voice assistant*

The new Hyundai VENUE will provide customers Enhanced Connectivity and enable them to control vehicle functions
right from the comfort of their homes. Customers can control multiple functions while also being able to check vehicle
status through Home to car (H2C) with Alexa & Google Voice Assistant. The features can be controlled with voice support
for English and Hindi Language.





Ambient sounds of nature

The new Hyundai VENUE will also boast of a soothing acoustic sound feature, with Sounds of Nature
offering customers a serene driving experience, be it in city traffic or on open highways.





60+ Bluelink connected features

The new Hyundai VENUE with Bluelink offers customers 60+ features enhancing their connected car
experience with heightened convenience and comfort.







10 Regional languages infotainment

To enhance convenience for customers from different parts of India, the infotainment system of the new
Hyundai VENUE will feature support 12 languages including 10 regional languages.





Connecting LED tail lamps

The rear section of the new Hyundai VENUE features unique first-in-segment Connecting LED Tail Lamps
with unique vertical design elements. The Connecting LED Tail Lamps stretch across the rear and
compliment the overall design with a futuristic and distinctive appeal while the hexagonal cut crystal
design creates a luxurious yet hi-tech appeal.





Drive mode select (Normal, Eco and Sport)

The new Hyundai VENUE has also been equipped with Drive Mode Select for a versatile driving
performance with the choice of Normal, Eco and Sport modes. With the choice of different drive modes
customers can enjoy a dynamic, sporty yet comfortable and economical driving experience.
